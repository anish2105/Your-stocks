Why "YOUR STOCKS" ?


Stocktake (or "inventory checking" is the physical verification of the quantities and condition of items held in an inventory) is an essential part of stock control and is the best way to keep track of your stock.

YOUR STOCK is an online-system for tracking items you have bought and sold.

YOUR STOCK helps you to record all stocks purchases you have made and view them at your convenience. It also helps in recording and tracking all the stocks you brought.

OUR MOTIVE?

Our website lets you view the total profit/loss you made throughout your stock trade journey so that you dont have to go through a rigorous process of calculating all the profit/loss and get a tally of it.

HOW TO USE?

  • Register your accout (its free).
  • Login with your account
  • Click on 'addstock' button
  • Start adding your stocks
  • If you want to update your stock:
  • Click on the update buton
  • If you want to delete your stock:
  • Click on the delete buton
  • Few things to remember:
  • While recording the selling price : add all price (i.e., selling price, broker charge and tax)
  • While updating , fill 'name of the stock' blank otherwise it will not be updated
